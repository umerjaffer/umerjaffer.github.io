Cookie Policy

This policy explains how we use cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience on our website.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience by:

  • Remembering your preferences
  • Understanding how you use our website
  • Improving our services
  • Providing relevant content

Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential Cookies Required for basic website functionality Session
Analytics Cookies Help us understand how visitors use our site 2 years
Preference Cookies Remember your settings and preferences 1 year
Marketing Cookies Track your online activity for targeted advertising 1 year

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our service
  • To notify you about changes to our service
  • To provide customer support
  • To gather analysis or valuable information so that we can improve our service

Managing Cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. You can delete all cookies that are already on your computer and you can set most browsers to prevent them from being placed. However, if you do this, you may have to manually adjust some preferences every time you visit a site.

Third-Party Cookies

We use services from third parties that may also set cookies on your device. These include:

  • Google Analytics for website analytics
  • Google Tag Manager for managing our tags
  • Social media platforms for sharing functionality

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
