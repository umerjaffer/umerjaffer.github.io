How to Convert Fractions to Percentages

Formula

(Numerator ÷ Denominator) × 100

To convert a fraction to a percentage, divide the numerator by the denominator and multiply by 100. This calculation expresses the fraction as a percentage of the whole.

Step-by-Step Example

Example: Convert 3/8 to a percentage
  1. Identify your values:
    • Numerator = 3
    • Denominator = 8
  2. Divide numerator by denominator:
    3 ÷ 8 = 0.375
  3. Multiply by 100:
    0.375 × 100 = 37.5
  4. Therefore, 3/8 = 37.5%

Tips for Accurate Conversions

  • Convert mixed numbers to improper fractions first
  • Use a calculator for precise decimal divisions
  • Round percentages to appropriate decimal places
  • Check that your answer is reasonable
  • Remember that proper fractions convert to percentages less than 100%
